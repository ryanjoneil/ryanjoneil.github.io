Hi, I'm Ryan.

I'm an analyst and coder by day, Ph.D. student in optimization by night, blogger, and amateur musician.

In my work, I guide and develop Zoomer's advanced mathematical capabilities. Our Decision Engineering team builds algorithmic components which integrate directly into real-time Logistics-as-a-Service (LaaS) systems.

My academic research applies optimization techniques to dynamic pickup and delivery problems under the direction of my advisor, Dr. Karla Hoffman. I'm also interested in cutting and packing problems, computational optimization, and modeling languages.
